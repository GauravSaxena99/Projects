About the Product

Leaving a moderately pungent taste on the tongue, Green capsicums, also known as green peppers are bell shaped, medium-sized fruit pods.
They have thick and shiny skin with a fleshy texture inside.



Benefits
Green capsicums have powerful antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties.
Rich in Vitamin A, B complex, C and phytonutrients. Relieves pain of bone disorders and has the capacity to relax the repirartory passage.

Storage and Uses
Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep capsicums dry as moisture makes them rot faster. Refrigerate the peppers unwashed, in a plastic bag and consume within a week. Resort to
refrigeration only when they cannot be consumed immediately.
Green bell peppers can be used for a wide variety of dishes due to its distinct flavour and scent. Can be grilled,roasted, baked and sauteed. Widely used in salads, pastas, pizzas, pepper sauce and even for
flavouring curries.
